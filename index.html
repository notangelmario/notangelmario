Poem for Andromeda


Did you know some stars are so hot they're blue?
Well some are so hot they start looking like you
There might be diamond planets out there in the space
But I still think in your arms is the most valuable place
Supernovas do so bright and beautiful a pattern
But to me those shiny eyes are everything that matters
Such a bummer that Galileo never found you
If he did he'd say the universe revolved around you


That heavenly body got all the stars outshined
So amazing you put all the shooting stars behind
Do you think it's hard to find beautiful constellations?
Well it's pretty hard to find such a beautiful creation
Always wanna put my grips on those hips
When i look at you gravity is just pulling me towards your lips
Do you believe in wonders cause I sure do
I don't want nobody else only me and you

(For Andreia, I'm grateful)
